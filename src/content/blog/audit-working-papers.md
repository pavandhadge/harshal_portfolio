---
title: "How I Build Audit Working Papers"
date: "2025-12-22"
summary: "A clear, step-by-step structure for documentation and review readiness."
tag: "Audit"
---

My working papers follow a simple structure: objective, scope, supporting documents, testing performed, and conclusion. This makes it easy for seniors to review and for me to trace any item later.

I also standardize naming and versioning, so the file trail remains clean during revisions.

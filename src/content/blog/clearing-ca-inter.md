Clearing CA Inter taught me that consistency beats intensity. I focused on short, repeatable study blocks, weekly revision, and solving past papers under time pressure. The biggest improvement came from learning to prioritize accuracy first and speed later.

I also kept a tight error log for Accounting and Taxation, which helped me avoid repeating the same mistakes. That habit now carries into audit documentation and compliance checklists.

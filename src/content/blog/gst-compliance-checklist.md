A clean GST workflow starts with matching invoices to purchase registers and reconciling GSTR-2B. I verify HSN/SAC classifications, ensure RCM entries are captured, and validate input credit eligibility before filing.

I also keep a simple reconciliation tracker so mismatches are resolved early, and filing deadlines are never rushed.
